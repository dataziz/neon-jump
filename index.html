Tutorial: Mastering the Nod

1️⃣ Set Up: Make sure your camera can see your face.

2️⃣ Get Ready: The game starts as soon as the camera connects.

3️⃣ Nod Down: Tilt your head down toward your chest.

4️⃣ Nod Up: Lift your head back up quickly to score!
